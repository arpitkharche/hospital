Dr. Ashutosh Kharche
MBBS, MD, Radiodiagnosis, FVIR

Dr. Ashutosh Dattatraya Kharche is an Endovascular Specialist practicing in the city of Mumbai. After completing his fellowship in Vascular and Interventional Radiology from Jawaharlal Nehru Medical College, he continued to work as Clinical Associate in Sir H N Reliance Hospital. During his tenure at Fellowship and Other Hospital he has been actively involved in treating complex endovascular cases along with Expert Interventional Radiologists. He has persued his MBBS from Mahaharashtra University of Health Sciences. He has several national and international publications to his credit. He is currently affiliated with Indian Society of Vascular & Interventional Radiology (ISVIR) and Cardiovascular & Interventional Radiology Society of Europe (CIRSE).

His area of expertise are:

  • Varicose Veins & Spider veins Management
  • Endovascular Management of Diabetic Foot
  • Deep Vein Thrombosis: Thrombolysis & IVC Filter Placement
  • Dialysis AV Fistula Angioplasty, Permcath Placement & Central Venous Obstruction
  • Endovascular Management of Aortic Aneurysm & Dissection
  • Uterine Fibroid Embolisation: Pelvic congestion Syndrome treatment
  • Radiofrequency Ablation of Liver Tumors and Osteoid Osteoma
  • Hepatobiliary: Percutaneous Biliary Drainage and stenting, Transjugular Liver Biopsy, TIPS, IVC Stenting and Hepatic Vein Stenting
  • Liver Cancer: Chemoembolisation (TACE), Radioembolisation (TARE)
  • Management of GI Bleed, Hematuria and Hemoptysis
  • Vascular Malformations Management
